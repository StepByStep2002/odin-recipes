Recipe for the Ukrainian Borscht

In this recipe we will look at how to make traditional Ukrainian Borscht

Here is the list of ingredients that we need:

  • Beef bones or beef broth
  • Beets
  • Cabbage
  • Potatoes
  • Carrots and onion
  • Tomato paste
  • White vinegar and sugar
  • Garlic
  • Dill
  • Bay leaves, salt and pepper
  • Sour cream

Let's get to cooking steps

  1. Prep veggies: You want to start with cabbage first because it takes the longest time to cook.

    While it is cooking, you can prep other vegetables.

  2. Cook cabbage in broth with bay leaves and peppercorns for 20 minutes after bringing to a boil. Chop beets, potatoes, carrots and onion in the meanwhile.with bay leaves and peppercorns for 20 minutes after bringing to a boil. Chop beets, potatoes, carrots and onion in the meanwhile.

  3. Saute onion and carrots in a bit of olive oil until translucent, about 5 minutes. This makes onion flavorful making entire borscht recipe more delicious. Do not skip.

  4. Then add beets and a bit more oil, cook for another 5 minutes. It’s called “zazharka”.

  5. Transfer sauteed veggies to the pot along with potatoes, tomato paste and salt. Cook covered for 20 minutes. In the meantime, prep garlic, dill and other seasonings.

  6. Season borscht with white vinegar, garlic, sugar and pepper.

    Stir, turn off heat and let it stand for 10 minutes covered to allow flavors to “marry” each other. Add dill and your borscht is ready to serve.
