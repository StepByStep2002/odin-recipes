Recipe for the pizza with olives

In this recipe we will look at how to make a delicious pizza with olives

This pizza will make for a great addition to any family gathering or party of friends

List of ingredients needed:

  • Easy no-knead pizza dough
  • Pizza sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pitted olives (any color)
  • Oregano
  • Chili flakes (optional)
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt & pepper

  5. Bake until golden and deliciously crispy. Enjoy!
