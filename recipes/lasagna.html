How to make a Lasagna

In this recipe we will bake a lasagna

Here is the list of ingredients that we need:

  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Spices
  • Garlic
  • Pasta sauce
  • Beef & Sausage
  • Mozzarella
  • Egg
  • Lasagna
  • Parsley
  • Tomato paste
  • Onion
  • Parmesan

Now let's get to steps to make Lasagna

  1. Boil pasta: In a large pot of salted water boil lasagna noodles

  2. Prepare meat sauce: Cook sausage and beef with onion and garlic. Drain well, add the pasta sauce (I love this brand) & simmer it for a few minutes to thicken.Cook sausage and beef with onion and garlic. Drain well, add the pasta sauce & simmer it for a few minutes to thicken.

  3. Combine cheese mixture: Stir the cheese mixture together in a bowl.

  4. Layer & bake: Layer the meat sauce and cheese mixture with lasagna noodles and bake until the top of the lasagna is golden brown.
